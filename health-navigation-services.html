Stanford Health Navigation Services is a customized web portal where all employees and their families can access specialist and secondary health resources, services, and amenities. From this site, you can also access the world-renowned Stanford Health Library, which provides scientifically based medical information to help you make informed decisions about health care.

Stanford Health Navigators are also available by phone to answer any questions and provide additional support and resources at times of important health care needs. Navigators can help with scheduling appointments and coordinating specialist visits at Stanford Hospitals and Clinics. Stanford Health Navigation Services are intended to complement the role of your current health care provider and primary care physician.
