Have a benefits issue you can't resolve? There's an advocacy service available at no cost that can help you and your eligible dependents navigate the health care system.

An Advocate can help you:

  • Understand your benefits paperwork
  • Obtain medication or treatment
  • Locate doctors and hospitals
  • Resolve health care billing and insurance claim disputes
  • Get answers to your questions about company-sponsored benefits

To reach an Advocate, call 888-622-1200888-622-1200 or visit alight.com/advocacy. Advocacy hours are Monday through Friday from 6:30 a.m. to 4:00 p.m., Pacific time.
